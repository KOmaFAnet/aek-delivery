About Us

Built to Deliver. Trusted by Restaurants.

At AEK Delivery, we believe that great food deserves fast and reliable delivery. Our mission is to help restaurants, cafés, and food businesses connect with their customers through a professional and efficient delivery service. We work as a trusted logistics partner for restaurants and food establishments that want to offer delivery without managing their own courier teams. By partnering with AEK Delivery, businesses can focus on what they do best — creating great food — while we take care of getting it to customers quickly and safely.Our team is dedicated to providing dependable delivery solutions, flexible service options, and a smooth experience for both businesses and their customers. Whether it's a small local café or a busy restaurant, we aim to support every partner with the same level of care and reliability. At AEK Delivery, we value speed, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is simple: make delivery easier for businesses and more convenient for customers.
